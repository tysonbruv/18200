LISTEN UP.

There are only two things you need to understand.

INCOME

Income is money in.

You sold a $5 sandwich today.
Someone paid you $5 into your bank today.

That is INCOME.

DEDUCTION

A deduction is money you spent on your business.

You spent $3 on bread, $2 on peanut butter, and $8 on a cool knife.

BOOM. Those are DEDUCTIONS.

You run a carpentry business.
You buy a $10 hammer, $20 tool belt, and a $200 Makita circular saw.

BOOM. All of those are DEDUCTIONS.
